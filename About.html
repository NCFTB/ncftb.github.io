About

BOINC

This project revolves around BOINC. In simple terms BOINC will allow smaller computers (such as the ones we use) to perform small tasks, which then contribute to a collective supercomputer usable for scientific means.

Bitney

As my School Bitney has supported me greatly. This project, is no exception. They have donated computers and power, along with space in a teachers room providing me my initial proof of concept and a contribution to science.

Systems

All systems run Lubuntu a lightweight Linux Operating System to decrease base system resource usage and allocate the most possible to BOINC. The systems are also all password protected and are kept locked to prevent the unlikely situation in which theft would occur.
